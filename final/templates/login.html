Rural Fund Management System

The Rural Fund Management System (RFMS) is designed to streamline financial management for rural development groups. It facilitates various operations to enhance efficiency and transparency within these groups.

Our system offers multiple modules to cater to different roles within the group, ensuring a comprehensive approach to fund management and reporting.

  • Registration

  • transaction management

  • reminders and refunds

  • fund tracking,transaction reports
